Tourism NZ Route Planner is an online tool which will help you calculate the approximate cost of your trip in New Zealand.

What it does

Our app works out your travel costs based on your input: travel days, amount of passengers, and choice of vehicle.

Please note

All costs and distances are approximate and should only serve as a basic guideline for planning your trip.
